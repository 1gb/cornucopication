Frequently Asked Questions

Why the name?

Intended to invoke the spirit of the bountiful harvest, we combined the word cornucopia with the word celebration and thus "Cornucopication" was born: a magical word that we hope fills your heart with hunger and joy. Cornucopication is a showcase for fun, food-oriented festivals and fairs that are meant to be enjoyed by all.

"Dream of Cornucopication."
-Eve Worthington,
Founder of Cornucopication

Perhaps you are looking for a fun family outing or a day-trip to a town you've never been to before. Whatever the reason that brought you to Cornucopication, we are very glad you're here. Please be welcome. You are family now.

"Don't judge each day by the harvest you reap but by the seeds that you plant."
-Robert Louis Stevenson

We at Cornucopication hope that you find our website useful and that you enjoy many wonderful trips to festivals in the years to come!


You're missing an event!

Cornucopication strives to maintain a list of fun, food-oriented, family-friendly events but we realize that sometimes we miss some! Currently we are limited to events in Washington state. If you have an event you'd like to see added to the site, send an e-mail to eve@eveworthington.com

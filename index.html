About

Why the name Cornucopication?

Intended to invoke the spirit of the bountiful harvest, we combined the word cornucopia with the word celebration. Together, these words form "Cornucopication", a magical word that we hope fills you with the joy of the season. We feel that small, local, family friendly festivals deserve to be showcased and enjoyed by all. Perhaps you are looking for a fun family outing, or a day-trip to a town you've never been to before. Whatever the reason that brought you to Cornucopia, we are very glad you're here. Please be welcome. You are family now.

"Don't judge each day by the harvest you reap but by the seeds that you plant."
-Robert Louis Stevenson

We at Cornucopia hope that you find our website useful, and that you enjoy many wonderful trips to festivals in the years to come!
